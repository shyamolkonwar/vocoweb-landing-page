## VocoWeb Landing Page Architecture // Specification v1.0

**Status:** Ready for Deployment
**Framework:** Sprint Zero / Fusion Focus Design System
**Objective:** High-conversion interface with strict "Fake Door" validation logic.

---

### 1. Global Layout Rules (The Grid)

* **Canvas:** `#FFFFFF` (White).
* **Container:** Fixed max-width `1152px`. Centered.
* **Grid System:** 12-column grid.
* **Separators:** All sections must be separated by full-width 1px solid borders (`#E5E5E5`).
* **Typography:**
* **Headings:** `Inter` / `Geist Sans`. Tracking: `-0.03em`. Color: `#0A0A0A`.
* **Body:** `Inter`. Tracking: `normal`. Color: `#525252`.
* **Metadata/Eyebrows:** `JetBrains Mono`. Uppercase. Size: `12px`. Tracking: `0.05em`. Color: `#525252`.


* **Corners:** Strict `0px` or `2px`. No pills.

---

### 3. Interaction & Telemetry Guide (Fake Door Logic)

This is the "Engine" of the page. The buttons are sensors, not just links.

**Object A: The Low Intent Sensor**

* **Trigger:** "Start Building" Button (Hero & Builder Plan).
* **Visual Response:** Modal Pop-up.
* Header: "ACCESS PROTOCOL".
* Body: "The VocoWeb Sandbox is currently at capacity. Enter email for priority access."
* Input: Sharp corners.


* **Data Event:** Log as `LEAD_CAPTURE_LOW_INTENT`.

**Object B: The High Intent Sensor**

* **Trigger:** "Start Business" Button (Founder Plan).
* **Visual Response:**
* *Option A:* Redirect to Stripe Test Mode Checkout.
* *Option B (Preferred):* Full screen form.
* Question 1: "Enter Business Name" (This validates they have an idea).
* Question 2: "Select Business Category".




* **Data Event:** Log as `PURCHASE_INTENT_HIGH`. This is your validation metric for the Merchant of Record feature.
